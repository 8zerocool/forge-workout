Refined workout plan

This keeps Gemini’s structure, but improves it for aesthetics by adding more side delts, upper chest, rowing, and a progression system.

Monday — Lower Body Power +
  • Box jumps — 3 sets of 5, soft landings.
  • Trap bar deadlift or front squat — 3 sets of 6–8.
  • Bulgarian split squat — 3 sets of 8–10 per leg.
  • Romanian deadlift — 3 sets of 8–10.
  • Weighted plank — 3 sets of 45–60 seconds.
Wednesday — Upper Body V-Taper +
  • Weighted pull-ups or lat pulldown — 4 sets of 6–8.
  • Incline dumbbell press — 4 sets of 8–10.
  • Chest-supported row or cable row — 3 sets of 8–12.
  • Overhead press — 3 sets of 8–10.
  • Face pulls — 3 sets of 15–20.
  • Lateral raises — 4 sets of 12–20.
Saturday — Full Body Athletic +
  • Dumbbell row — 3 sets of 8–10 per side.
  • Weighted dips or bench press — 3 sets of 8–10.
  • Goblet squat or leg press — 3 sets of 10–12.
  • Incline cable fly or incline machine press — 3 sets of 10–15.
  • Farmer’s walks — 3–4 rounds.
  • Optional lateral raises — 2–3 sets of 15–20.
Sunday — Longevity + Weak Points +
  • Turkish get-ups — 2–3 sets of 3 per side.
  • Hanging leg raises — 3 controlled sets.
  • Reverse curls — 3 sets of 12–15.
  • Triceps rope pushdowns — 3 sets of 12–15.
  • Single-leg calf raises — 3 sets of 12–15.
  • Deep stretching or yoga-style mobility — 10–15 minutes.

Biggest upgrades

These changes make the plan more aligned with an aesthetic, athletic, long-term physique.

🦾

More side delts

Add lateral raises 2–3 times per week. Side delts create the wide shoulder look.

🧱

More upper chest

Incline press plus incline fly or incline machine work creates the squared upper torso.

🏹

Add rows

Rows balance pressing, improve posture, and help build a thicker athletic back.

📈

Progression system

Use double progression: hit the top of the rep range, then increase weight and repeat.

Abs are built by the cut

You probably do not see abs because of body fat, not because you need endless ab exercises. The cut reveals the muscle you already have.

Recovery rules

  • Do not take every lifting set to failure.
  • Most sets should stop with 1–2 reps in reserve.
  • Keep Sunday lighter so Monday legs stay strong.
  • Deload every 5–6 weeks if joints, sleep, or performance decline.
  • Fuel kickboxing days with enough carbs and hydration.
