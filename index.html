TODAY'S PURPOSE: You trained chest and back tomorrow — today is active recovery in the morning and fat-burning combat cardio in the evening. Kickboxing burns fat nothing else can replicate. This is where your body fat comes off fastest. Show up.
🥊
FIGHT NIGHT
Kickboxing 3× per week is the most powerful fat-loss tool in your program. A single session burns 500–700 kcal, improves cardiovascular fitness, builds coordination, and — critically — preserves muscle in a way steady-state cardio cannot. You are not just losing fat. You are becoming athletic.
🍽️
Pre-Session Nutrition
Eat a balanced meal 90–120 min before class. Carbs + protein. Avoid training on empty — you'll gas out fast. Soy protein shake if short on time.
💧
Hydration
2 glasses of water 1 hour before. Bring a water bottle. Kickboxing sweat rates are high — dehydration kills both performance and recovery.
🧠
Mindset
Be a beginner without ego. Focus on technique over power. Every session your coordination and cardio improves — track it.
🛡️
Equipment
Hand wraps + gloves are mandatory. Ask the gym if they provide them initially. Proper wrapping protects your wrist joints long-term.
❄️
Post-Session
5 min cool-down stretch. Hit protein target within 45 min. Cold shower optional but excellent for reducing inflammation.
😴
Sleep Priority
Evening training can delay sleep. Wind down 60 min before bed — no screens, dim light. 7–9h sleep is non-negotiable for fat loss + recovery.

💤
RECOVER & GROW
Muscle is not built during training. It's built during rest. Protein synthesis peaks 24–72 hours post-workout. Today is not a wasted day — it is when the adaptations you're working toward actually happen.
😴
Sleep 7–9 Hours
Your single highest-leverage recovery tool. Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep. Nothing replaces this.
🥛
Hit Protein
125–140g even on rest days. Muscle repair doesn't pause. Paneer, tofu, soy protein, lentils, Greek yogurt.
💧
Hydrate
3–4 litres. Connective tissue, tendons and joints require hydration to repair properly overnight.
🚶
Light Walk OK
10,000 steps walking is fine and encouraged. Not a workout — daily human movement.
🧠
Manage Stress
Chronic cortisol kills muscle and drives fat storage. Protect this day. Read, rest, do nothing.
🌅
Morning Sunlight
10 min outside within 30 min of waking. Sets circadian rhythm, improves sleep quality tonight.
⚠️ Deload Reminder
Every 4–6 weeks, take a full deload week. Reduce all working sets by 40–50% while keeping the same weight. This is critical on a calorie deficit when recovery is already reduced. Signs you need a deload early: persistent joint soreness, strength dropping over 2+ sessions, loss of motivation, poor sleep despite rest.
